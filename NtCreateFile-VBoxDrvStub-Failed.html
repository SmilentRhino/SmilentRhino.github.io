Virtualbox problem, reason unkown, solve by rebooting.

As air conditioner in our previous office is broken, we moved to another office nearby. Everything is fine except that my vagrant boxy doesn't work properly. Than I have to turn on the GUI in vbox manager, and here is the error info.


NtCreateFile(\Device\VBoxDrvStub) failed: 0xc000000034
STATUS_OBJECT_NAME_NOT_FOUND (0 retries)

Driver is probably stuck stopping/starting. Try 'sc.exe query vboxdrv' to get more information about its state. Rebooting may actually help. (rc=-101)

Make sure the kernel module has been loaded successfully.

After rebooting, it work fine now. Why the error occur, and why rebooting fix it? I will check it later
