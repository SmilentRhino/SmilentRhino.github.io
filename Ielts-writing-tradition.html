This practice is about tradition.

Many customs and the traditional ways of behavior are no longer relevant to the modern life, and not worth keeping. Do u agree or disagree?

In modern society, it is inappropriate or even weired to act in certain ways like what we did before. In my opinion, some of these traditions should be totally abandoned, while there are still some need to be perserved because they have become part of our culture.

On one hand, traditions which are against human rights or related to superstition should not exist in the beginning and should be eliminated from our society immediately. First, in such a modern world where gender equality is widely accepted and promoted, force females to bind there foot to show off their social status is ridiculous and against their basic rights. Second, choose to neglect the most advanced medical system in human history to ask a witch docter for help when a person have mental problem is totally in vain and may deeply harm the patient's health.

On the other hand, traditions like family gather at Christmas in western countries or on Spring Festival in China are more a cultural behavior and beyond the gather itself. People need such customs to share a collective identity and strengthen a sense of community. No matter how conviniently we could connect with each other on social network provided by advanced technology, this gather is still important and irreplaceable.

In conclusion, we should decide whether or not to keep a tradition based on the behavior itself. As long as it is benefitial for our culture and health, it should be kept, and not vice versa.
