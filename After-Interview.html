Here comes the end of my job seeking, I deeply understand my weakness and so-called advantages, I never lose my confidence, but I see more clearly now.

Advantages

  1. I never stop thinking. I have foster a habit to examine our daily operation and system in different aspects. I never underestimate the importance of automation, high availablity, efficient backup and reliable monitoring.
  2. I still have the enthusiasm for new technology.
  3. I am still lazy and optimistic. I am still eager to automate everything and believe in automation.
  4. I know programs, not like so many other operators, who never wrote a single program, I understand mvc, I know architecture, I have the idea of class, and all that about single step debugging.
  5. I want to solve problem. I am a person how love challenges so much.

Weakness

  1. I think a lot but do little. Whether it is because of the platform or my personality, I haven't achieve that much so far. I wouldn't start until I get the pefect design, but you know, in fast-moving IT industry, nothing is pefect.
  2. I am greedy. I always chase new shiny techonoly without knowing all the details. My flippancy always block my way.
  3. I have lost my modesty. My colleagues often think in a pratical way, and do better in something, but I refuse to learn from them, in fact, I pay so little attention to what they are on. Even though I see the drawbacks of their solution and know how to improve it, I offer little help.

What next

  1. In the next year, I will complete a project with more then 5 functions and 10000+ lines.
  2. I will read my books on the ipad one by one.
