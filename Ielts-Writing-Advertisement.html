The twelveth writing practice, advertisement

Question

Advertising industry has a huge influence on the society. Do you think positive effects of advertising outweigh negative effects

My Essay 1

People always see advertisements about all kinds of products everywhere anytime in modern society, willingly or not. Some think they provide us an efficent way to filter better goods out, while others criticise the falsehood and misleading they bring. I will discuss both opinions and explain why advertising industry still benifits our world more.

First, good advertisement plays an irreplaceable role in marketing, for they have existed for thousands of years and evolve with the world. Ever since we changed rice with meat, people have gotten a deep understanding for reputation, the magic power of advertising in convince people to buy more goods. As the industry grows so complex, people need a sophiscated and efficent method to help them make choices. Undoutfully, the better advertisement always means, if not all, a health financial status, only giants in the industry can afford a series of well-designed attractive advertisements. What's more, these commercials or leaflets highlight what people should focus on, and keep customer from bewildering by some shinny but useless functions provided by low-quality goods, though quite a few big companies also apply this strategy sometimes.

However, uncontrolled advertisements do not not distrub the customers, but also hinder the development of other business. For example, too much popup messages from the right bottem corner on your computer never fail to distract people from the task they are on, no matter what content they contain. What is worse, false advertisements may cause a widespread crisis of confidence in the industry, financial support may decrease after the lost of faith.

In conclusion, though false and uncontrolled advertisement have some negative impact on society, we still have a strong need for a healthy advertising industry to get the right information and promote our business.
