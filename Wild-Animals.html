The nineth ielts writing practice, protect wild animals.

Some people argue that too much attention and too many resources are given to the protection of wild animals and birds. To what extent do you agree or disagree?

Ideas

  1. Biodiversity provides critical support for drug discovery and the availability of medicinal resources.
  2. Wild animals promotes agriculture production.
  3. China is returning farmland to forest and grass.
  4. These conserved land can be use to produce more food, we haven't eliminate hunger
  5. Funding for panda is a waste of money.
  6. People misunderstand natural slection, it doesn't mean to let them extinct.

My Essay 1

It is argued that our government and wildlife protection organisations have paid too much attetion in protecting wildlife and wasted a lot of scarce resources. However, in my opinion, we need to take more effort instead of to cut the cost to save these precious animals due to their irreplacable role in ecosystem.

First, more animals are dying out despite of our investment. Though a large amount of money have been poured into wildlife protection and thousands of acres of land have been spared for endangered species, species are going extinction at a much faster rate then they did before. It is an undeniable fact that human activities are the major cause of the damage of wild habitats, nature reserves all the world seems little compared with their lost lands under global warming.

Second, the benifits brought by biodiversity are far beyond the little investment we put in animal protection. Wild animal plays an pivotal role in keeping the balance of biodiversity, the whole system might crash because of the extinction of one single spiecy of bird, for their pray could ruin all the plants. Our agricultre production are fragile under the invasion of insects without the help of wild birds. What's more, wild animals can provide a support for the availability of medicinal resources. Drugs discovered and developed from animal have saved millions of human lifes.

In conclusion, wild animals are so important to the existance of human being that we can't let them become extinct due to our own faults. More efforts are to be taken to maintain the biodiversity on earth.
