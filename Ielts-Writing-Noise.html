The seventh practice

Some people think that the amount of noise people make have to be controlled strictly. Others say that people are free to make as much noise as they wish. Discuss both views and give your opinion.

Ideas

  • Noise harm people's health, hearing loss, high blood pressure, ischemic heart disease, sleep disturbances, injuries, and even decreased school performance.
  • Noise can damage psychological health.
  • Noise can harm the balance of animal life.
  • People are free to make sound in there private space.
  • Normal activies such as music practive, baby crying, pet barking cause noise.
  • Regulation protect people from overly sensitive neighbor's complaints.
  • Noise is mainly caused by machines and transportation systems, motor vehicles, aircraft, trains and construction activities.
  • Noise can be caused by poor urban planning.

My Essay 1

While many goverments have set regulations to protect citizens from adverse sound level exposure, some advocates treat it as violation of basic human rights and argue that we should defend our freedom to make sounds. In my opinion, reasonable rules can protect people from negative effects cause by noise pollution and allow people to live together harmoniously.

To start with, exposure to noise is harmful to both human and animals. First, noise creates not only annoyance, but also significant health consequences such as elevated incidence of hearing loss and cardiovascular disease. And noise pollution may also damage psychological health problem such as hypertension or anxiety. What's more, noise have a detrimental effect on wild animals, increasing the risk of death by changing the delicate balance in predator or prey detection and avoidance.

On the other hand, some people argue that they have the right to make sounds as they like, at least in their own private space. The sound produced by music instruments practice, which is often regarded as noise, is unavoidable. And it is natural for their lovely dogs to bark when happy or curious, as long as not they do not bark excessively, we should not force them to be silent.

In my opinion, most strict regulations we made are targeted at noise produced by construction activities or transportation systems. As long as one do not make constant unwanted loud sound in the midnight such as throwing a midnight party, little of our nomal life would be affected. In fact, people can deternime if the complaint they recieved from a oversensitive neighbor is valid according to city noise ordinance, thus better protect their own freedom.

Useful Sentences

  • Noise pollution may cause or contribute to the following adverse effects: anxiety, stress, nervousness, nausea, headache, emotional instability, argumentativeness, sexual impotence, changes in mood, increase in social conflicts, neurosis, hysteria, and psychosis.
  • While noise-induced hearing loss is permanent, it is also very preventable.
  • In animals, noise can increase the risk of death by altering predator or prey detection and avoidance, interfere with reproduction and navigation, and contribute to permanent hearing loss.
  • High noise levels can contribute to cardiovascular effects in humans and an increased incidence of coronary artery disease.
  • Noise is a social issue and we need rules in society to allow people to live together harmoniously.
  • Roadway noise can be reduced by the use of noise barriers, limitation of vehicle speeds, alteration of roadway surface texture, limitation of heavy vehicles, use of traffic controls that smooth vehicle flow to reduce braking and acceleration, and tire design.
