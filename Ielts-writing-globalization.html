This writing practice is about globalisation.

In many countries, People can eat a wide variety of food today. As a result, they eat food from other regions than local food. Do you think the advantages of the development outweigh disadvantages?

Thanks to ongoing globolization all over the world, food to enjoy nowadays is no longer limited in domestic. While bringing economic benifits and culture exchanges, importing food from other place . This essay will discuss merits and demerits of this development, and give reasons why it's far more

Advantages of this development are not limited in economy but also culture. First, food imports contributes to job creation. The increased economic activity associated with every stage of the food import process helps support thousands of jobs. Sencond, competition brought by food from different regions will urge local food makers seek for improvement in both product quality and working environment. What's more, food imports play an important role in intercultural exchange. For example, Chineses cuisine is another reason beside Kongfu why western youth are facinated by this mysterious country.

However, the development still come with a price. Small restaurants often find it hard to compelete with global food gaints. Limited advertisemnet budget constrained their influnce on young children when facing companies which are endorsed by pop or athelete stars.

飞速发展的科技和逐步完善的物流使地球显得越来越小，人们可以轻而易举的品尝异国美食, 即使远隔重洋。但是并非所有人都是这种进步的拥趸， 有些人担忧他们会对本地的经济和文化产生强烈的冲击。 在我看来，食物的交流其实是文化交流的一种形式与媒介，其利大于弊。

异国美食增进了人们的物质生活，也引起了大家对其背后文化的兴趣。首先对于内陆地区， 来自海岛的美味海鲜往往让人无法抗拒，居住在温带地区的人们往往热衷于热带地区的水果。 其次，不通的菜肴，比如中国的饺子，日本的寿司，作为各自文化的代表， 吸引西方游客对东方文明进行进一步的探索，这种交流是双向的，异地美食走进来的同时， 本地食物也将走出去。

跨国美食，通常由食品巨头掌控，会对本地饮食行业产生影响。本地规模较小， 管理不善的食品企业，确实会面临经营成本的压力。但这也是一个产业升级的机会， 学习竞争对手的供应链管理和营销策略，也会帮助他们提升自己的品质。 真正的美食并不会消失，最有可能是并入这种新的供应链。

综上所述，这种进步极大的促进了文化的交流，对于本地食品行业既是挑战，更是机遇， 我们应更积极的拥抱这种改变，享受其带来的便利。
