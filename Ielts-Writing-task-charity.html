The first practice is about charity.

Some people think that charity organisations should only offer help to people of their own country. But others believe that these organisations should give aid to people in great need wherever they live. Discuss both views and give your opinion.

My Essay1 It is argued whether focus of charity organisations should be limited at home or should they reach out to people who are in worse situation abroad. In my opinion, all human lives should be valued equally, international charity activities are necessary to build the common future of the human race. This essay will discuss both views.

Charity organisation should help neediest people despite of nationalities. Most charity organisations are funded in developed countries, where social programs are adequate to keep people from being in a despair situation. Whereas in the third wolrd, hunger and poverty still haunts young children and elders. Food or medicine from nonprofit organization could mean a matter of life or death to many of them. For example, an anti-malaria bed net costs only $2.50 and protects two people for up to three years.

However, antagonists highly doubt the efficiency of giving money to people living in other country. Generally, most charitable nonprofits spend between 10 to 30 percents on overhead. Difficulties in management significantly raise the expenses in daily operations, which might result in more than 50 cents per one dollar being spent outside the real programs. Fortunately, authorities can reduce unnecessary expense in the long run after enough experience was accumulated. If new techonogies and method are properly applied in management, cost of international aids will be no more than local ones.

In conclusion, while allocating money to a different nation potentially increase burdon on management, charitable organization must take the responsibility to assist people in need.
