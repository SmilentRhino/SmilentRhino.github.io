The tenth ielts writing practice.

Many museums and historical sites are mainly visited by tourists, not local people. Why is this the case? What can be done to attract local people?

Ideas

  1. Local people have been there once.
  2. Visitors are more attractive to a new culture.
  3. Local people don't have enough motivation, they think they can go there any time.
  4. Most museums are targeted at more generous foreign tourists.
  5. A year membership to local residents.
  6. Special exhibits, tours and workshops for kids.
  7. Borrow items from other museums to lure local people.
  8. Price of the entrance ticket is often too high for local inhabitants to affod.
  9. Local people are busy compared with tourists from tourists.

My Essay 1

Compared with the enthusiastic tourists, local people seem to show indifference to place of interests in their hometown despite the convinience and relative lower cost. This essay will examine the reasons for this before suggesting some possible methods of attracting local people.

Firstly, while visitors are driven by curiosity about a different culture, local peole lack this kind of motivations. Local people are familiar with the history either through their textbooks or from the story their family tell, thus they are unlikely to gain the exotic experience and feel as thrilled as the tourists do. Secondly, tourists from all over the world set aside time and save money for the visits they could pay only once in a lifetime, local citizens don't want to spend too much time or money on such activies which they can do anytime in their life, even thought the cost is relatively much lower.

However, it doesn't mean museums and historical sites are of no interests and not important to local people. Rotating exhibitions of borrowed items from other museums are a huge draw for local people. Another way is to offer more special exhibits, tours and workshops for kids, parents who are eager to enrich their children's life always would not hesitate to pay a visit. It's also a great way to strengthen family bonds and build community culture.

In conclusion, diffrent motivations are the major factor why tourists are more willing to visist historical places and museums. Cooporation between museums and special culture events can help to promote attendance of native residents.
